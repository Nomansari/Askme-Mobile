About Mercado

Mercado Amusement Park was conceived and developed by Seth Groups — a five-year flagship project focused on sustainable development, immersive themed experiences, and family entertainment. The park covers 45 acres and integrates modern ride engineering with themed landscaping.

Development Timeline
  • Year 0: Concept & Master Plan
  • Year 1-2: Groundworks & Infrastructure
  • Year 3-4: Attractions construction & Testing
  • Year 5: Soft launch, staff training, and grand opening

Seth Groups remains actively involved in park operations, investments in guest experience, and community programs.
